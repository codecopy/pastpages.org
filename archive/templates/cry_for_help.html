This is my cry for help.

Hello world. Pas­t­Pages needs your support.

I cre­ated this site be­cause I think it ought to ex­ist. The shift­ing homepages of ma­jor me­dia sites should be saved so they can be stud­ied. Done right, I be­lieve Pas­t­Pages could serve as a re­source for schol­ars seek­ing to study cov­er­age of news events, like the up­com­ing U.S. pres­id­en­tial elec­tion.

Reg­u­larly col­lect­ing this data costs money. Right now I'm pay­ing for it out of pock­et. My back of the en­vel­ope math suggests it will cost at least $60 each month to keep alive, even at low traffic levels. I will not be able to af­ford that for long, especially if traffic levels increase and drive up costs.

This is my cry for help. I think this site be­longs in a lib­rary where it can be avail­able to scho­l­ars and me­dia crit­ics. If you can help, please contact me at ben.welsh@gmail.com.

In the meantime, I am accept­ing do­na­tions via Kickstarter. If you would like to give, click the box below. If you kick in there are some re­wards, and I'll even add your site to the index, assuming it's le­git­imate news. If I don't meet my goal all the mo­ney is lost, so please help make this hap­pen.

Thank you.
